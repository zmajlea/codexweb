The Chief of Staff of every venture

CodexOne is not another folder or signature tool. It is a governance engine—an active system that runs your rules. It enforces role-based permissions, orchestrates approvals, seals changes, and keeps an auditable memory of how your venture evolves.

Governance Engine Highlights

  • Role-centric permissions (Founder, Admin, EA, Officer, Board, Advisors, Legal, Investors, Shareholders, Registered Agent).
  • Sealing flow with thresholds (e.g., majority founders, all board) and immutable audit trails.
  • Lifecycle controls: Draft → Pending Seal → Sealed → Superseded.
  • Delegation, revocation, and structured metadata for every artifact.

Artifacts: the building blocks

Each entity maintains sealed, versioned, and auditable artifacts representing the venture’s living record— founder agreements, cap table snapshots, bylaws, IP assignments, advisory grants, and more.

  • Structured IDs and metadata, linked supporting documents.
  • Governed updates after sealing; everything logged.
  • APIs and AI extraction (planned) to surface clauses and obligations.
